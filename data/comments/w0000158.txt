comment = The following comment is submitted in response to concerns that ambivalent language may become the basis for introduction of GEO's into organic foods. To prevent this possibility, the language in section 205.2 should be amended (see capitalized words) to state:  205.2   Treated. A seed, plant propagation material or other material purchased for use as a production input in an organic farming or handling operation that has been GENETICALLY ENGINEERED, treated or combined with a synthetic pesticidal substance (that does not appear on the National List) prior to having been purchased.
